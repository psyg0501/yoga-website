Yoga and Health

Yoga is an ancient practice rooted in Indian philosophy. It began as a spiritual practice but has become popular as a way of promoting physical and mental well-being. Although classical yoga was founded on spiritual elements, popular yoga as practiced these days consists of physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation (dhyana).

There are many different yoga styles, ranging from gentle practices to physically demanding ones. For optimum benefits, an individual set of practices should to be designed to fit the physical condition of the practitioner.
